Chicken Road is an exciting lane-crossing game by Evoplay where you guide a chicken across 5 lanes of traffic. Each successful crossing increases your multiplier, but getting hit by a car means losing your bet. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to start playing.

Game Overview

Chicken Road puts a casino twist on the classic "why did the chicken cross the road" concept. Your goal is simple: cross as many lanes as possible without getting hit by traffic, all while deciding when to cash out your winnings.

The 5-Lane Road

Lane 5 - Final (Hardest) Up to 1000x
Lane 4 High Multi
Lane 3 Medium Multi
Lane 2 Low Multi
Lane 1 - Start (Easiest) Starting Multi

Step-by-Step Guide to Playing

1

Set Your Bet Amount

Before starting, choose how much you want to wager. Use the + and - buttons or enter a specific amount. As a beginner, it's wise to start with smaller bets until you're comfortable with the gameplay.

2

Start the Game

Click the "Play" or "Bet" button to begin. Your chicken will be positioned at the starting point, ready to cross the first lane. Your bet amount is now locked in for this round.

3

Cross the First Lane

Click "Cross" to attempt crossing the first lane. The outcome is determined by the Random Number Generator (RNG). If successful, you'll see your multiplier increase and can proceed to the next decision.

4

Decide: Cash Out or Continue

After each successful crossing, you face a crucial choice: cash out your current winnings or risk them by attempting the next lane. This decision is the core of Chicken Road's gameplay.

5

Repeat or Collect

Continue crossing lanes and making decisions until you either cash out, successfully cross all 5 lanes for the maximum multiplier, or get hit by a car and lose your bet.

Understanding Game Outcomes

Successful Lane Crossing

When you successfully cross a lane:

  • Your multiplier increases
  • You advance to the next lane
  • You can choose to cash out or continue
  • The next lane becomes available to cross

Getting Hit by a Car

If your chicken gets hit while crossing:

  • You lose your entire bet for that round
  • The round ends immediately
  • All accumulated multiplier is lost
  • You can start a new round with a fresh bet

Cashing Out

When you choose to cash out:

  • You receive your bet multiplied by the current multiplier
  • The round ends successfully
  • Winnings are added to your balance
  • You can start a new round

The Decision Flow

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start playing Chicken Road?

To start playing, set your bet amount using the controls, then click the Play or Bet button. Your chicken will be positioned at the start, and you can begin crossing lanes by clicking the Cross button.

When should I cash out in Chicken Road?

You can cash out after any successful lane crossing. The decision depends on your risk tolerance - cashing out early is safer but offers lower multipliers, while continuing risks your entire bet for higher potential rewards. There's no "right" answer - it's personal preference.

What happens if my chicken gets hit by a car?

If your chicken gets hit while crossing a lane, you lose your entire bet for that round. The game then resets and you can place a new bet to try again.

Is there a way to guarantee wins in Chicken Road?

No. Chicken Road uses a certified RNG, meaning outcomes are completely random. The 96% RTP ensures the house always has a mathematical edge. No strategy can guarantee wins.

How many lanes do I need to cross for maximum win?

You need to successfully cross all 5 lanes to achieve the maximum multiplier of 1000x. This is statistically rare, which is why the reward is so high.
