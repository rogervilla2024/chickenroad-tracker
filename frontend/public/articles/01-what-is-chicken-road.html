Understanding the Chicken Road Concept

How Chicken Road Works

The gameplay mechanics of Chicken Road are elegantly simple yet deeply engaging:

The 5-Lane System

1 First Lane - Lowest risk, lowest multiplier increase
2 Second Lane - Increased risk and reward
3 Third Lane - Medium risk, medium reward
4 Fourth Lane - Higher risk, higher reward
